Parents’ room
Everything is so tidy. It’s the parents’ room. It seems that they’ve been here a short while ago. There are three different chairs in the room: blue, yellow, and green. ‘They don’t fit the room’, the detective thought. He sits on each of the chairs. Nothing happens. He knows he should change his view. He moves the chairs. One of them has a loose leg. He detatches it, and a message appears above the bed.
